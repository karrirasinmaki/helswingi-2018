                      TERMS & CONDITIONS

                      Helswingi festival 2018 (later "Event" or "Festival") terms and conditions. The Event is organised by Black Pepper Swing and Swing jam (later "Organisers" or "Organiser").

                       
                      Registration

                       

                      Registration is binding. After confirmed registration, cancellation is not possible.

                       

                      Discounts

                       

                      In case of financial struggles, separate discounts and payment arrangements may be allowed. Please contact us directly via email at info@helswingi.fi.

                       

                      Payment

                       

                      The registration fee has to be paid within 14 days after confirmed registration. You will receive payment info via email. Interest for late payment may apply (penalty interest rate, 7%/year).

                       

                      Schedule and content changes

                       

                      Unfortunate and surprising changes regarding schedule, teachers and other contents of the Event are informed mainly on this website. 

                       

                      Photography and videography

                       

                      The Event will be documented - photographs and video will be taken. By taking part in the Event you grant the Organisers full rights to use the images resulting from the photography/video filming, and any reproductions or adaptations of the images and video for marketing, publicity or other purposes. This might include (but is not limited to), the right to use them in printed and online publicity, social media, press releases and advertisements.

                      If you do not wish to be photographed please inform the Organisers.

                       

                      Other

                       

                      All sort of disturbing behaviour and sexual harassment is forbidden in and during the Event. If the student causes damage to the property of the venues, he/she will be held responsible to pay the damage caused. A person breaking these disturbing behaviour rules may be removed from the Event.

                      If you see or hear anything that disturbs you or might disturb others, please contact us directly via email at or mobile +358442644912.
