                      HELSWINGI

                      On 5 - 6 - 7 October 2018 it is time to swing up the Finnish capital.

                      Join us to learn with international instructors, stay up late and dance to the best Finnish swing bands at fantastic venues. With 2 days of classes and 3 nights of parties, there is plenty of time to get connected, appreciate autumn in Helsinki and burn holes into your shoe soles.

                       

                      • Lindy hop, Collegiate shag, and Balboa tracks 

                      • International teachers

                      • Friday, Saturday and Sunday party

                      • Renowned swing bands

                      • Beautiful, historical venues

                       


                      Helswingi is an authentic swing dance festival held in Helsinki, Finland. This event is organized by Black Pepper Swing and Swing jam!

                      Teachers

                      10 teachers from 8 different countries teaching collegiate shag, balboa and lindy hop.
